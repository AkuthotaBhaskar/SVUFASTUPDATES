What do you mean by Result?


Something that results as a consequence, issue, or conclusion. also : beneficial or tangible effect : fruit. : something obtained by calculation or investigation.

Has result in meaning?

Too cause (something) to happen. The disease resulted in his death. : to produce (something) as a result. The trial resulted in an acquittal.
